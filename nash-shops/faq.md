# FAQ

**Q: Shop interaction is not appearing.**
A: Ensure ox_target is running and shop coords are correct.

**Q: Items are not in the player's inventory after purchase.**
A: Ensure ox_inventory is running and the item names match your items table.

**Q: Can I add custom shop types?**
A: Yes, add new entries to `Config.Shops` with unique names and item lists.

**Q: How do I enable the robbery mechanic?**
A: Set `Config.EnableRobbery = true` and configure the robbery settings in config.lua.

**Q: What currencies are supported?**
A: Cash and bank account payments are supported via the Config.Currency option.
