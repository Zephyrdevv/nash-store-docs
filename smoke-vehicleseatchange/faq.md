# FAQ

**Q: How do I change the keybind?**
A: Edit `Config.Keybind` in config.lua to any valid FiveM key name.

**Q: Does this work in all vehicles?**
A: Yes, it works with cars, trucks, boats, and helicopters. Planes may have limited seat options.

**Q: Can passengers switch seats too?**
A: Yes, any player inside the vehicle can use the seat change feature.

**Q: Will this conflict with other vehicle scripts?**
A: No. This script only handles seat switching and does not modify vehicle behavior.
